Nutrition Menu

  • The menu provides an average of 2469 calories per day. You may need a few hundred calories more or less, depending on your size, age, and activity level. You can go to www.choosemyplate.gov to estimate your calorie needs and get more nutrition information.
  • The menu is omnivorous—it includes all kinds of foods—and is as balanced as possible. A vegetarian menu could be just as affordable and nutritious.
  • Use condiments, like fresh or dried herbs, spices, or soy sauce or hot sauce, to add variety and give meals some kick! Condiments are not included in cost estimate.
  • Note that all meals and snacks are prepared at home. Preparing food at home yes, even cooking dry beans will take a little time and effort, but is a lot cheaper than eating in restaurants.

Low-Carb Menu

Sample menu of the diet during the week.

It provides less than 50 grams of total carbs per day. However, if you are healthy and active, you can eat more carbs.

  • Monday

    • Lunch: Grass-fed yogurt with blueberries and a handful of almonds.
    • Dinner: Bunless cheeseburger, served with vegetables and salsa sauce.
  • Tuesday
    • Breakfast: Eggs, and Bacon.
    • Lunch: Veggies and Leftover burgers.
    • Dinner: Salmon, butter and vegetables.
  • Wednesday
    • Breakfast: Eggs, vegetables, deep fried in butter.
    • Lunch: Salad with Shrimp and little olive oil.
    • Dinner: Grilled chicken, and vegetables.
  • Thursday
    • Breakfast: Omelet with different kind of vegetables, deep fried in coconut oil or butter.
    • Lunch: Smoothie with Avocado, Berries, Almonds.
    • Dinner: Steak with veggies.
  • Friday
    • Breakfast: Bacon and eggs.
    • Lunch: Salad, Chicken and some olive oil.
    • Dinner: Pork chops and various vegetables.
  • Saturday
    • Breakfast: Omelet, different kind of veggies.
    • Lunch: Yogurt with Berries, Flakes and walnuts.

  • Sunday
    • Breakfast: Bacon and eggs.
    • Lunch: Smoothie, chocolate, protein powder and berries.
    • Dinner: Grilled chicken wings, and raw spinach.
